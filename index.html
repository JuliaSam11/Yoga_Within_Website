Our classes

Hatha


A beautifully balanced practice of physical postures, energetic and breath techniques and guided relaxation or meditation. This class will get you moving and breathing and leave you feeling both energized and grounded. Suitable to those with some level of fitness but beginners are welcome.

Power yoga


Power yoga is a dynamic and invigorating yoga practice, always moving safely and mindfully with your breath. Power yoga enthusiasts say it enhances stamina, flexibility, posture, and mental focus. Like all physical activities, it also relieves tension and releases toxins through sweat. Because it is rigorous, it burns more calories than most traditional forms of yoga and therefore can help with weight loss.

Vinyasa


The variable nature of Vinyasa Yoga helps to develop a more balanced body as well as prevent repetitive motion injuries that can happen if you are always doing the same thing every day.
